About lemon water

"Lemon water was reserved for fine dining just a generation or two ago, served alongside every diners' place setting at an embellished dining table. Now infused waters are available almost everywhere, and Millenials have it for breakfast", says dad.

My dad frequently uses the 'lemon water' we Millennials order to drink (or so he has observed as an Executive Chef) as a metaphor for the millennial mindset. It's his nickname for my sister and I when we say something that embodies a go-getter, I can do anything mentality. As such, 'lemon water' frequently accompanies the discussions we have about my career transition from a Product Marketer to a Software Engineer.

This blog is meant to share my experiences transitioning to and growing in this new career with others interested in switching careers and too starting over with something fresh mid-career.
