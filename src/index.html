About lemon water

"Lemon water was reserved for fine dining just a generation or two ago, served alongside every diners' place setting at an embellished dining table. Now infused waters are available almost everywhere, and you Millenials have it for breakfast", says dad. He uses the 'lemon water' we Millennials order to drink (or so he has observed as an Executive Chef) as a metaphor for the millennial mindset. It's his nickname for my sister and I when we say something that embodies that go-getter, I can do anything mentality, that sometimes seems too outside the norm to my parents' and previous generations. 'Lemon water' frequently accompanies the discussions we've had about my transition from a Product Marketer to a Software Engineer. This blog is meant to share my experiences transitioning to and growing in this new career with those who are darish enough to switch careers and start over with something fresh.
